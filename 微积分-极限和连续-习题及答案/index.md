   * *[2, 3]*
     $$
     \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \frac{f(x_2) - f(x_1)}{x_2 - x_1} = \frac{(3^3 + 1) - (2^3 + 1)}{3 - 2} = 3
     $$

   * 

   * *[-1, 1]*
     $$
     \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \frac{f(x_2) - f(x_1)}{x_2 - x_1} = \frac{(1^3 + 1) - ((-1)^3 + 1)}{1 - (-1)} = 1
     $$

2. *$ R(\theta) = \sqrt{4 \theta + 1} $*

   * *[0, 2]*
     $$

     $$
